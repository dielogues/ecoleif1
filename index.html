FOR CHANGEHOW WE WORK

Scan and Sort

Scan and sort your packages at home. Plastic containers, milk cartons, metal cans – anything with a barcode can be recycled with our app. Simply scan those barcodes as you prepare your recycling.

STEP:1

Add Recycling Locations

Add your existing recycling locations. Whether it's your home recycling bins or local recycling facilities, input them into our app for easy access.

STEP:2

Track Your Recycling

Track your recycling progress. Monitor the amount of waste you recycle and the impact you're making on the environment through our app's tracking feature.

STEP:3

Earn Rewards

Earn rewards for your efforts. As you continue to recycle, accumulate points that can be redeemed for discounts, vouchers, or even cash rewards.

STEP:4

Engage with Community

Engage with like-minded individuals. Connect with other users in the community, share tips and tricks, and participate in challenges to further promote recycling efforts.

STEP:5

Make a Difference

Make a positive impact on the planet. By recycling with our app, you're not only earning rewards for yourself but also contributing to a cleaner and healthier environment for future generations.
